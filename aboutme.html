About Me

I founded my tutoring company two years ago at the age of 19. Throughout my middle school years, I struggled with Math. During that time I used to go online for help or I would find a private tutor in my area from online websites to help me with my classes. Some of the tutors I had were not qualified or the background in teaching. However, there were other times I found great tutors that helped me turn my low grades into A's or B's in the course. As I began to see a change in my grades I began to find a love for math. This is when I realized there was a lack of qualified tutors in my area. This is when I decided to start MathBeats to help other students
